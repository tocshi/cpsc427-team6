Everything looks cool.

Interaction is not super smooth. Requires to click on "go back" to the selection menu for the next interaction. I think a more user friendly way to do the interaction is to use left mouse click for accept and right mouse click for cancel. 

It is difficult to determine the attack range of the player and the mobs. Need to get fairly close to the mobs in order to attack them. 

Say energy gauge is 100 and attack consumes 50. If I am the player I probably would move for 50 energies and then attack to maximize my turn. If I moved a little bit more say 51 energies,  then I cannot perform the attack, which is sad  ;(. 
My thought on the attach system is to scale the attack power of the attack based on the energy left in the gauge. You can perform a strong attack if you didn't move at all, or a weak attack if you travelled for a long distance. But this restrict the player from moving after performing an attack as a trade off.

The game was great but I find it difficult to have to press a button before moving or attacking and it is pretty confusing to end turn by pressing a button.
